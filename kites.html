Brief history of kites

Kites have a long and fascinating history that dates back over 2,000 years. The first kites were invented in China during the Han Dynasty, where they were used for military purposes such as signaling and measuring distances. Over time, kites became popular for recreational purposes and were used in festivals and competitions. In the 18th and 19th centuries, kites were used for scientific experiments, including weather forecasting and studying electricity. Today, kites are enjoyed by people of all ages and can be found in a variety of shapes, sizes, and designs. From traditional diamond-shaped kites to high-tech stunt kites, there is a kite for everyone to enjoy.

Importance of kites in different cultures

Kites have been an important part of various cultures around the world for centuries. In China, kites were used for military purposes and were believed to bring good luck and ward off evil spirits.
